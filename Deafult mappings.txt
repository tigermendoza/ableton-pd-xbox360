Xbox 360 control pad to Ableton Live with Pure Data 

Default mappings

Inroduction
These are the Xbox controller to midi mappings used in the sample project and the Pure Data scripts.

Note: All cc and note values use midi channel 13.

Analogue controls:
Left thumb stick up: 		1
Left thumb stick right: 	2
Left thumb stick down: 		3
Left thumb stick left: 		4

Right thumb stick up: 		5
Right thumb stick right: 	6
Right thumb stick down: 	7
Right thumb stick left: 	8

Left trigger:				9
Right trigger: 				10

Buttons:
A button:					E3
B button:					F3
Y button:					F#3
X button:					G3
LB button:					G2
RB button:					G#2
Back button:				A2
Start button:				A#2
